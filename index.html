About US

35YEARS OF EXPERIENCE


In Dry Cleaning

Chennai Dry Cleaners is a full-service dry cleaners since 1990, We started our career by providing laundry services to the most reputed institution of India, IIT Madras in the year 1990. After 15 years of service and experience with many educational institutions,star hotels and other commercial institutions, we decided to offer dry cleaning cum laundry services to people in residential areas and hence started our dry cleaning cum laundry shop at Adayar, Chennai where we are servicing the entire satisfaction of all of our customers and public who are residing in the surrounding areas of Besant nagar such as Shashtri nagar, Gandhi nagar, Adayar, Thiruvanmiyur.etc Due to the growing demands of laundry services and to give respect to the request of our customers, We have decided to extend our services to all areas in Chennai through our free collection and delivery service. With our free collection and delivery service, our committed staff will be at your door steps and all you have to do is just call us and let us know about your laundry needs. We collect clothes from your home and deliver back clean and shining. You can save your precious time. To keep your wardrobe looking and feeling its best, we implement the latest, most effective dry cleaning and laundering technologies, backed by decades of hands-on experience and ongoing training in stain removal and restoration. Our aim is to provide our customers with the best dry cleaning and laundry services by understanding their laundry needs & preferences.

Our Services


Dry Cleaning

We offer a comprehensive dry cleaning service for every day items such as suits, dresses, trousers and more. We do dry cleaning with a solvent of international choice - Perc(Perchloroethylene) which is absolutely harmless to the fabric. With our 2-3 day service your dry cleaning will be ready to wear before you know it. Our collection and delivery service conveniently calls at your home or office.


Wet Cleaning

With our Laundry Services you can look forward to your clothes being delivered to your door freshly laundered, crisply pressed and ready to wear. All your clothes are taken personal care and the nature of the cloth is clearly examined before washing in order to wash them with appropriate fabric friendly detergents. After washing, all clothes are pressed to give a crisp and neat finish to it. Call us and we will pick.


Saree Polishing

Sarees, especially silk sarees tend to lose their brightness and shine in the course of time as you use it. We offer best saree polishing service which preserves the inherent shining of sarees without affecting its natural beauty and durability. It is good to give a combined treatment of dry cleaning plus saree polishing to make your sarees look equal to brand new ones. Call us and we will pick up your sarees at your door step.


Blazer Dry Cleaning
With our years of experience, CDC has perfected the cleaning, pressing and packaging of suits, so you receive them clean, crisp, and wrinkle free. Once you will experienced a CDC Blazer Dry Clean you will keep coming back! Experience a perfectly pressed garment, hand finished by one of our experienced pressers. You will see and feel the difference. Express Delivery is also available!
